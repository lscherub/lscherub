about me

i'm a digital designer in love with art, culture and discovering new worlds. i enjoy making simple and beautiful interface designs. it all started with writing silly pieces of code and combining them to try something unique. i love what i do and adore the creative process.

i love music, travel, and coding. when i'm not coding, you'll find me working on a personal project (which i usually end giving up on, haha). other than that, i like taking photographs, making music, reading poetry and browsing reddit.

collaborating on a project is a real treat, so if you have a project in mind, feel free to say, "Hi!".
